Detailed Description

Computes statistics in inertia properties of considered shape.

Author
Benjamin Bercovici
Jay McMahon

Computes the standard deviation and covariances in volume, center of mass, inertia tensor, inertia moments, principal dimensions and principal axes. Interfaces with ShapeUQLig. Note that this class will always shift the origin of the provided shape's coordinates to the shape's barycenter

MIT License, Benjamin Bercovici and Jay McMahon

Computes the inertia statistics of the provided shape using a Monte-Carlo sampling of the control point deviations Refer to "Inertia of An Uncertain Small Body Shape" by Benjamin Bercovici and Jay McMahon, Icarus, 2019 for further details

Parameters
N_samplesnumber of samples in the monte carlo
lcorrelation distance (L). Governs correlation decay
sigmastandard deviation of normal uncertainty (L)
output_dirdirectory where to save "slices" taken from the different shapes generated in the monte carlo. Only 20 slices are generated. If argument is left to default "" string, then nothing is saved
